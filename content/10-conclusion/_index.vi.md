# Ket luan va Buoc tiep theo

## Tom tat Domain

Chuc mung ban da hoan thanh huong dan on thi SAA-C03 toan dien! Ban da hoc het 4 domain:

**Domain 1: Thiet ke kien truc bao mat (30%)**
- IAM users, groups, roles va policies
- MFA va federation
- Bao mat VPC voi Security Groups va NACLs
- Ma hoa at rest (KMS) va in transit (ACM)
- Cac dich vu bao mat AWS (WAF, Shield, GuardDuty, Macie)

**Domain 2: Thiet ke kien truc phuc hoi (26%)**
- Auto Scaling va load balancing
- Loose coupling voi SQS, SNS, EventBridge
- High availability voi Multi-AZ
- Chien luoc disaster recovery va RPO/RTO

**Domain 3: Kien truc hieu suat cao (24%)**
- Giai phap luu tru (S3, EBS, EFS, FSx)
- Toi uu compute (EC2, Lambda, containers)
- Hieu suat database (Aurora, DynamoDB, ElastiCache)
- Gia toc mang (CloudFront, Global Accelerator)

**Domain 4: Toi uu chi phi (20%)**
- Tuy chon mua EC2 (Reserved, Savings Plans, Spot)
- S3 storage classes va lifecycle policies
- Chien luoc toi uu chi phi database
- Ky thuat giam chi phi mang
