# Huong dan on thi AWS Solutions Architect Associate (SAA-C03)

## Tong quan

Ky thi AWS Certified Solutions Architect - Associate (SAA-C03) danh gia kha nang thiet ke giai phap dua tren AWS Well-Architected Framework. Workshop toan dien nay bao gom ca 4 domain cua ky thi voi ly thuyet, bai thuc hanh, de thi thu, cheat sheets, flashcards va bang so sanh.

## Cau truc ky thi

- **Dinh dang:** 65 cau hoi (50 tinh diem + 15 khong tinh diem)
- **Loai cau hoi:** Trac nghiem mot dap an va nhieu dap an
- **Thoi gian:** 130 phut
- **Diem dat:** 720 / 1000
- **Chi phi:** $150 USD

## Cac domain va trong so

| Domain | Trong so | Mo ta |
|--------|----------|-------|
| Domain 1 | 30% | Thiet ke kien truc bao mat |
| Domain 2 | 26% | Thiet ke kien truc co kha nang phuc hoi |
| Domain 3 | 24% | Thiet ke kien truc hieu suat cao |
| Domain 4 | 20% | Thiet ke kien truc toi uu chi phi |

## Muc tieu hoc tap

Sau khi hoan thanh workshop nay, ban se co the:

- Thiet ke chien luoc truy cap bao mat su dung IAM, VPC va cac dich vu ma hoa
- Kien truc giai phap co kha nang phuc hoi cao voi chien luoc DR phu hop
- Lua chon giai phap luu tru, tinh toan, co so du lieu va mang hieu suat cao
- Toi uu chi phi tren tat ca cac loai dich vu AWS
- Ap dung AWS Well-Architected Framework vao cac tinh huong thuc te
- Tu tin tra loi cac cau hoi SAA-C03 tren tat ca cac domain

## Cach su dung huong dan nay

Moi trang task trong workshop bao gom:

1. **Ly thuyet** — Giai thich chi tiet ve cac dich vu va khai niem AWS
2. **Bai thuc hanh** — Bai tap thuc te tung buoc voi AWS CLI va Console
3. **Flashcards** — Cap cau hoi-tra loi de on nhanh
4. **Cau hoi thi thu** — Cau hoi thuc hanh theo dinh dang ky thi voi giai thich
5. **Tai lieu tham khao** — Lien ket den tai lieu AWS, blog va whitepapers

Lam theo thu tu cac domain, hoac nhay den cac chu de cu the ban can on tap. Su dung phan thi thu de danh gia muc do san sang.
