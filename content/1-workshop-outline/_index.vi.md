## Tong quan Workshop

Workshop nay la huong dan on thi toan dien cho ky thi AWS Certified Solutions Architect - Associate (SAA-C03). Noi dung duoc to chuc theo cac domain va task statement chinh thuc cua ky thi.

## Thong tin ky thi

- **Ma ky thi:** SAA-C03
- **Cap do:** Associate
- **Kinh nghiem khuyen nghi:** 1+ nam thuc hanh voi AWS
- **So cau hoi:** 65 (50 tinh diem, 15 khong tinh diem)
- **Loai cau hoi:** Trac nghiem mot dap an (1 dung trong 4) va nhieu dap an (2+ dung trong 5+)
- **Thoi gian:** 130 phut
- **Diem dat:** 720 tren 1000
- **Mo hinh cham diem:** Bu tru (khong can dat diem tung phan rieng le)

## Cau truc ke hoach hoc

Workshop duoc chia thanh cac phan sau:

### Phu song Domain (14 Trang Task)

| Phan | Domain | Tasks | Trong so |
|------|--------|-------|----------|
| Domain 1 | Thiet ke kien truc bao mat | Task 1.1, 1.2, 1.3 | 30% |
| Domain 2 | Thiet ke kien truc phuc hoi | Task 2.1, 2.2 | 26% |
| Domain 3 | Thiet ke kien truc hieu suat cao | Task 3.1, 3.2, 3.3, 3.4, 3.5 | 24% |
| Domain 4 | Thiet ke kien truc toi uu chi phi | Task 4.1, 4.2, 4.3, 4.4 | 20% |

### Cac phan bo sung

- **Bang so sanh** — So sanh cac dich vu AWS thuong bi nham lan
- **Cheat Sheets va Flashcards** — Tai lieu tham khao nhanh
- **De thi thu** — 50+ cau hoi theo trong so domain
- **Ket luan va Meo thi** — Chien luoc ngay thi va buoc tiep theo
- **Tai nguyen** — Lien ket den tai lieu AWS, blog, whitepapers va khoa hoc

## Thu tu hoc khuyen nghi

1. Doc qua tung domain theo thu tu (Domain 1 den Domain 4)
2. Hoan thanh cac bai thuc hanh khi hoc
3. Xem lai bang so sanh va cheat sheets
4. Lam de thi thu de xac dinh diem yeu
5. Quay lai cac trang task cu the dua tren ket qua thi thu
6. Lam lai de thi thu cho den khi dat tren 800 diem
