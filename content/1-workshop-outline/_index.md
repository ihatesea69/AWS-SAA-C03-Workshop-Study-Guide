## Workshop Outline

This workshop is a comprehensive study guide for the AWS Certified Solutions Architect - Associate (SAA-C03) exam. It is organized to follow the official exam domains and task statements.

## Exam Information

- **Exam Code:** SAA-C03
- **Certification Level:** Associate
- **Recommended Experience:** 1+ year hands-on with AWS
- **Question Count:** 65 (50 scored, 15 unscored)
- **Question Types:** Multiple choice (1 correct out of 4) and multiple response (2+ correct out of 5+)
- **Duration:** 130 minutes
- **Passing Score:** 720 out of 1000
- **Scoring Model:** Compensatory (no need to pass each section individually)

### Additional Sections

- **Comparison Tables** — Side-by-side comparisons of commonly confused AWS services
- **Cheat Sheets and Flashcards** — Quick-reference materials for rapid review
- **Mock Exam** — 50+ questions weighted by domain to simulate the real exam
- **Conclusion and Exam Tips** — Exam-day strategies and next steps
- **Resources** — Curated links to AWS documentation, blogs, whitepapers, and courses

## Navigation Guide

Use the left sidebar to navigate between sections. Each task page contains:

1. **Theory** — Core concepts and AWS service details
2. **Hands-On Lab** — Practical exercises with step-by-step instructions
3. **Flashcards** — Quick Q&A pairs for review
4. **Mock Exam Questions** — Practice questions with detailed explanations
5. **References** — Links to official AWS documentation

## Recommended Study Order

1. Read through each domain in order (Domain 1 through Domain 4)
2. Complete the hands-on labs as you go
3. Review the comparison tables and cheat sheets
4. Take the mock exam to identify weak areas
5. Revisit specific task pages based on your mock exam results
6. Retake the mock exam until you consistently score above 800
