## Practice Exam Providers

### Recommended Platforms
- **AWS Skill Builder** — Official AWS practice exams
- **Tutorials Dojo** — High-quality practice exams with detailed explanations
- **Whizlabs** — Practice tests and hands-on labs
- **A Cloud Guru** — Comprehensive courses with labs

### Practice Exam Tips
- Take multiple practice exams from different providers
- Focus on understanding explanations, not just memorizing
- Identify weak areas and review corresponding topics
- Aim for consistent scores above 80% before the real exam

## Study Schedule Recommendations

### 8-Week Study Plan
- Weeks 1-2: Domain 1 (Security) + hands-on labs
- Weeks 3-4: Domain 2 (Resilience) + practice questions
- Weeks 5-6: Domain 3 (Performance) + Domain 4 (Cost)
- Week 7: Review weak areas, cheat sheets
- Week 8: Mock exams and final review
